Minicraft: Ultimate Edition

Minicraft was made by @notch for the 22'nd Ludum Dare competition in December 2011.
Minicraft: Ultimate Edition is a mod being developed in @ItsYourPalMike's spare time.

The main purpose of the mod is to allow additional content to be seamlessly added into the game. This is accomplished with a lovely little plugin system. A plugin is a tiny .jar file that contains new mobs, items, tools, weapons, resources, sounds, graphics, menus, world generation code, etc. Pretty cool, huh?

When the game starts you can pick which plugins will be enabled during the session. Official plugins developed by myself or approved authors are available through the in-game downloads menu. Alternatively, plugins can be added by simply dragging the .jar file into your .minicraft folder.

What is Minicraft?

Minicraft is a game that was made in a 48-hour time period for the Ludum Dare Competition. You might know the creator of Minicraft from his similarly-named, slightly more famous game, Minecraft! The theme of the contest was "alone", and Notch decided to make a survival game where the end goal is to kill the only other sentient being in the world, making sure you'll be alone forever. You must explore, mine, collect resources, and craft tools to be prepared to take on the Air Wizard!

You can check out the original Ludum Dare entry here: CLICK ME!

That was back in 2011, and since then there's been a handful of mods, most of which can be found here: PlayMinicraft.com

How to play?

Controls: Arrow Keys, X, and C

Walkthrough

Originally from the Minicraft Wiki Walkthrough Page

After you familiarize yourself with the controls and the inventory and energy systems, the three main things you will want to start gathering are apples, wood, and slime. So, go ahead and punch trees until you get about 30 or so apples, and however much wood comes with the apples when the trees break. While you are punching trees, however, remember to kill all the slime balls you see so you can collect their slime, which is a key ingredient in making lanterns. (Lanterns are very important) Don't forget to replenish any health you use by selecting apples from your inventory, then eating them with C. (Watch your energy bar when doing this)

Rock Tools: Next, you will want to make rock tools. To do this, you can first place a workbench, then craft a wood pick and start hitting rock, OR you may simply hit the rock with nothing equipped. Either way, keep destroying rock until you get 5 pieces of rock. Go back to your workbench and craft a rock pick. Now, go back to whatever rock you were hitting and get more rocks. 30 rocks later, return to your workbench and craft a rock shovel, a rock sword, and a furnace.

Note: If you should need to move any workbenches, furnaces, lanterns etc., this is what the power glove is used for. You can equip it to lift and re-place your equipment. (Or put it back into your inventory)

Lanterns: Your next goal is going to be to make a few lanterns; I would recommend making around four lanterns, which requires 16 sand, 10 slime, 5 wood and 4 coal per lantern, so equip your rock shovel and find a sandy area. Dig about 48 to 80 tiles of sand, then mine enough coal to use up most if not all of the sand. Now you'll need to cut down trees and find slimes to loot. The last things we need to get are wood and slime, so get 30 to 50 slime and 15 to 25 wood. Once you're finished collecting the resources, place a furnace, make 12 to 20 glass panes, then place a workbench and craft 3 to 5 lanterns. Put the furnace and the workbench back in your inventory.

Now comes the tricky part...

Staircases: You'll need to wander around the map until you find a staircase going up that is surrounded by a bluish rock, if you don't know if it's the right staircase, make sure you cannot break the rock surrounding the staircase. Next, remember where this staircase is and then wander around it until you find another staircase, except this one should be leading down and surrounded by breakable rock. Remember where these two staircases are relative to each other.

Level 1: OK it's now time to make your descent into the underground, level 1. Mine your way to the staircase going down, equip your lantern, and enter. Once down there, put your lantern down on the staircase you are standing on. This will be your staircase-marking lantern. Now kill any enemies you find with your sword, remembering to put your lantern down beforehand. Replenish any health lost with apples. Now, take another lantern out and look for gray spikes. When you find them, don't touch them, just start mining them with your rock pick. Watch your energy. Once you have 60 iron ores, collect your non-staircase-marking latern and return to the surface. Make sure you have 15 pieces of coal, 20 rocks and 10 pieces of wood. Put down your workbench and furnace. Use the furnace to make 15 iron bars. Use the workbech to make an anvil, place the anvil, and make an iron pick and an iron sword on it.

Level 2: OK now it's time to get to the underground, level 2. In order to find the second level, go down the first stair case, then travel to the location relative to where you found the blue-rock staircase in the overworld. There, you should find another saircase leading down. Welcome to level 2. Place a lantern on the stairs, then look around for some gold spikes, and mine them until you have 40 gold ores. Go back to your furnace and make 10 gold bars, then use those to make a gold pick and a gold sword on the anvil.

Level 3: Time for level 3. Go down to level 2, then travel to the location relative to the level 1 staircase. There, you should find yet another staircase leading down. Welcome to level 3. Be careful of the lava, as it inflicts 4 hearts of damage to the player. Now look around for some purple spikes. Mine 100 gems with your gold pick, and get back to your anvil. Use the gems to make a gem sword and gem pick.

Sky World: We are now ready to fight the Air Wizard. Stock up on apples and then use your gem pick to get rid of the bluish stone around that staircase in the overworld you found earlier. Equip your gem sword and go up the stairs.

Welcome to the Sky World. In order to beat the Air Wizard, have a look at the Air Wizard page.
